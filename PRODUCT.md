## Users

Founders, small business owners, and teams that need a dependable full stack developer to ship landing pages, internal systems, automations, integrations, and digital products without heavy process overhead.

## Product Purpose

This portfolio presents Luan Cunha as a senior, pragmatic full stack developer with almost 8 years of experience. It should quickly build trust, show technical range, communicate business value, and drive qualified WhatsApp conversations.

## Brand Personality

Technical, direct, hacker-minded, confident, precise, and practical. The voice can borrow from terminal and Matrix culture, but the result must still feel professional enough for a business buyer.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic resume pages, corporate template sameness, playful gamer aesthetics, overloaded neon effects, identical card grids, and vague claims without proof of execution.

## Design Principles

1. Lead with technical confidence before biography.
2. Make the Matrix mood feel intentional, not costume-like.
3. Translate full stack breadth into business outcomes.
4. Keep conversion paths obvious and low-friction.
5. Use motion and atmosphere to add presence without hurting readability.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-friendly navigation, responsive layouts, clear focus states, semantic sections, readable Portuguese copy, and reduced-motion alternatives for animated effects.
